Rooted in Beauty & Balance

At Petal Pal, we believe that food should be as beautiful as a garden and as refreshing as a deep breath. Founded by a young entrepreneur with a vision for flourish aesthetics, our bakery is a space where art and health come together. We don’t just bake; we create edible masterpieces designed to make you feel good from the inside out.

Our Signature Craft

Every Petal Pal creation starts with one idea: balance.

The Floral Roll

Our signature cinnamon rolls are hand-sculpted into intricate flower shapes. We use premium cinnamon and natural ingredients so every “petal” pulls away with the perfect texture—a classic treat, reimagined as a blooming work of art.

◎

Zen-Cream

Ice cream, reinvented for the modern, health-conscious soul. Zen-Cream is crafted with wholesome, natural ingredients—a creamy, chilled experience without heavy processed sugars. Dessert that honors your body.
